ABOUT US

Agriconnect is a non profit initiative to eliminate food wastage and help farmers sell their produce as well as sell it on this platform it self. We connect the farmers with the warehouse as well as have a logistic provider to facilitate transportation to the warehouse

Agriconnect is a non-profit initiative committed to tackling the critical issue of food wastage and empowering farmers by providing them with an efficient platform to sell their produce. Our primary mission is to establish a sustainable ecosystem that seamlessly connects farmers, warehouses, and logistics providers, ensuring that harvested crops reach their destination with minimal waste.

At Agriconnect, we believe in harnessing the power of technology to streamline the agricultural supply chain. By establishing direct connections between farmers and warehouses, we aim to eliminate unnecessary intermediaries and ensure that farmers receive fair prices for their produce. Through our platform, farmers can not only sell their crops but also access essential resources such as warehousing and transportation services.

Key Features of Agriconnect include facilitating a direct link between farmers and warehouses to enable seamless transactions and reduce post-harvest losses. Our network also includes logistic providers who play a crucial role in transporting produce from farms to warehouses, ensuring timely and efficient delivery. By optimizing the supply chain and providing farmers with necessary resources, Agriconnect actively contributes to minimizing food wastage and promoting a more sustainable food system.
